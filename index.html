MUJKAN Family

History behind Stolarija MUJKAN

Stolarija "MUJKAN" is a proud, family-owned business with a tradition spanning four generations. Passed down from father to son, our passion for woodworking has brought warmth, beauty, and functionality to countless homes and spaces. From custom furniture to large-scale projects, every piece we create reflects our dedication to quality, precision, and heart.

Over the decades, we have crafted a wide range of furniture and contributed to notable projects that have become cherished landmarks. These include the medžlis in Tešanj, the elegant šadrvan in front of the Foča džamija, and the careful renovation of the Tuzla Old Square džamija. Each project blends our deep respect for tradition with our ability to deliver modern, lasting craftsmanship.

For fifty years, we relied on word-of-mouth to grow our business. While this approach built our reputation, we now embrace technology to expand further. By creating a website, we aim to share our story, showcase our craftsmanship, and demonstrate the endless possibilities we can achieve with wood, reaching more people and continuing our legacy.
